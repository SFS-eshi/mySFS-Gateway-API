Hosted Tokenization

Hosted tokenization is a feature that allows for an iFrame of sensitive payment fields (i.e., credit card details) to be placed on the merchant’s website or software.

These data fields are hosted by mySFS and not the merchant and greatly minimizes the scope of PCI compliance without disturbing the UX/UI or redirecting the customer to a separate page.

Hosted Payment Form

Add a link to your website or email invoice to redirect your clients to a hosted payment page
